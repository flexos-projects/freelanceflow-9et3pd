# Main Dashboard Page Specification

This specification details the main Dashboard page for FreelanceFlow, serving as the primary landing page after a user logs in. The Dashboard is designed to provide freelancers with an immediate, high-level overview of their most critical business metrics and activities. It aggregates information from various parts of the application, including projects, invoices, and tasks, to present a concise summary that helps users quickly assess their workload and financial status. The goal is to enable efficient decision-making and prioritize actions.

## Key Components & Information Displayed

*   **Project Summary:** A widget displaying the number of active projects, upcoming deadlines, and potentially overdue projects. Links to the full Projects list.
*   **Invoice Summary:** A widget showing pending invoices, total outstanding amount, and recently paid invoices. Links to the Invoices section.
*   **Task Overview:** A list of upcoming or overdue tasks, possibly with quick action buttons to mark as complete or view details.
*   **Revenue Snapshot:** A quick view of recent earnings or projected income.
